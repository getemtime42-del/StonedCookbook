Info & Conversions

This App's Standard Batch

Every recipe assumes you are infusing 14 grams of flower at 20% THC into 16oz (1 lb) of your chosen base using the double boiler method.

Total THC in a standard batch: 2,800mg. Per oz: ~175mg. Per tbsp: ~11mg. Per tsp: ~3.6mg. Start with half a teaspoon if new to edibles.

THCA Conversion

Using THCA Flower

THCA converts to active THC when heated during decarboxylation. You lose approximately 12-13% in the process.

Formula: THCA% × 0.877 = effective THC%

THCA%Effective THC% after decarb
20%~17.5%
25%~21.9%
30%~26.3%
35%~30.7%
Decarb THCA flower exactly like regular flower: 240F for 40 minutes. After that, treat it identically.
Concentrates

Wax, Shatter & Earwax

Concentrates are already decarboxylated. Skip the oven step. Dissolve directly into warm base in the double boiler.

TypePotencyPer 16oz base
Wax / Budder60-80%0.5-1g
Shatter70-90%0.4-0.8g
Earwax / Crumble60-80%0.5-1g
Live Resin65-85%0.5-0.9g
  1. Warm base in double boiler to 160-180F.
  2. Add measured concentrate directly to warm base.
  3. Stir gently 5-10 minutes until fully dissolved.
  4. Remove from heat, cool, strain if needed.
  5. Store and use exactly like flower-infused base.
1 gram of 80% shatter = 800mg THC. Start with 0.25g and scale up once you know how your batch affects you.
